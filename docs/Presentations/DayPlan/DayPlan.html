Activity 2 - Designing a Forecasting Contest

How its going to work!

  • Stage 1 - Introduction to Forecasting Contests (10 minutes)
    • Quick presentation from the SPHERE-PPL team on how the forecasting contests are going to work mechanically and what makes a good contest
  • Stage 2 - Break-Out Discussions (40 minutes)
    • Split into random teams of 5
    • Each team takes pens and A3 sheet
    • Pick a challenge area from the next slide and design a forecasting contest
      • Include an exam question and a brief summary of the topic
      • Think about the type of data that might already exist that participants could use
      • What will the outputs look like?
      • Is the context easy to pick up for a contest participant?
  • Stage 3 - Group Feedback (40 minutes)
    • Each team will give an overview of their proposed contest
    • Indicative vote on what would provide greatest impact
